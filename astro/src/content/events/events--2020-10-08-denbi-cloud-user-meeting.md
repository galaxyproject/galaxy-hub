The **3rd de.NBI Cloud User Meeting** will take place online for the first time. There will be talks and workshops by experienced users and long-running projects related to cloud computing. The EU Galaxy team will give a workshop in BioConda and Biocontainers on Thursday the 8th of October.

Five days to get ready to scale up your analysis! Find more workshops in the [program](https://cloud.denbi.de/3rd-de-nbi-cloud-user-meeting/).
