title: Freiburg Galaxy Services
---

The Freiburg Galaxy Team provides open infrastructure and support for
reproducible data analysis.

## European Galaxy Server

[UseGalaxy.eu](https://usegalaxy.eu/) provides public access to Galaxy tools,
workflows, reference data, compute resources, and storage. See the
[Galaxy Europe overview](/eu/) for service information and the
[storage guide](/eu/storage/) for current quota and data-management options.

## Training

The team contributes to the
[Galaxy Training Network](https://training.galaxyproject.org/) and supports
hands-on workshops. Trainers can request dedicated compute resources through
[Training Infrastructure as a Service](https://usegalaxy.eu/tiaas/new/).
Upcoming activities are listed on the [Freiburg events page](/freiburg/events/).

## Data Analysis And Tool Integration

The team collaborates on scientific data analysis, Galaxy tool integration,
workflow development, and reproducible software packaging. Public tools and
workbenches are listed in the [Galaxy platforms directory](/use/).

## Research Infrastructure

Galaxy services are supported through collaborations with the
[University of Freiburg](https://uni-freiburg.de/),
[de.NBI](https://www.denbi.de/), [ELIXIR](https://elixir-europe.org/),
and European open-science projects.

For service support, use the channels listed on the
[Galaxy Europe support page](/eu/about/).
