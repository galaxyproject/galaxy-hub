---
title: Freiburg Galaxy Projects
---

The Freiburg Galaxy Team contributes to Galaxy infrastructure, software,
training, and open-science projects in Germany, Europe, and the wider Galaxy
community.

## Galaxy Community

- [Galaxy](/): core development, public infrastructure, and community support.
- [UseGalaxy.eu](/eu/): operation and development of the European Galaxy server.
- [Galaxy Training Network](https://training.galaxyproject.org/): open training
  materials and training infrastructure.
- [Intergalactic Utilities Commission](/iuc/): integration and maintenance of
  scientific tools.
- [Bioconda](https://bioconda.github.io/) and
  [BioContainers](https://biocontainers.pro/): reproducible software packaging
  used by Galaxy tools.

## European Infrastructure

- [EuroScienceGateway](/projects/esg/): federated access to data, tools,
  workflows, and compute resources through Galaxy.
- [de.NBI](https://www.denbi.de/): German bioinformatics infrastructure,
  training, and cloud computing.
- [ELIXIR Galaxy Community](https://elixir-europe.org/communities/galaxy):
  coordination across ELIXIR nodes and scientific communities.
- [DataPLANT](https://nfdi4plants.org/): research data management for plant
  science, including Galaxy storage integration.

## Research Software

The team develops and maintains Galaxy tools and workbenches for areas including
RNA bioinformatics, genome annotation, epigenetics, imaging, climate science,
and data management. Browse the [Galaxy platforms directory](/use/) for public
servers and domain-specific workbenches.
