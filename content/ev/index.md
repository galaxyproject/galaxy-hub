Galaxy turns Essenial Variables from an abstract acronym into operational processes. Across the public usegalaxy.* services, researchers can access and / or produce 
Essential Variables. We propose here to centralize and showcase Galaxy oriented materials communities can use linked to Essential Variables concept, through Essential 
Climate Variables and Essential Biodiversity Variables.
