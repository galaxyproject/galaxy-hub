The imaging flavor of Galaxy offers a filtered set of tools that you can assemble into workflows to manage image data, and perform image analysis and processing.

# Partners

The imaging flavour of Galaxy is a joint effort between different partners including EOSC-Life and its associated Research Infrastructures ([Euro-Bioimaging](https://www.eurobioimaging.eu/), [ELIXIR](https://elixir-europe.org/)), [NFDI4BIOIMAGE](https://nfdi4bioimage.de/), [BMCV (Heidelberg University)](http://www.bioquant.uni-heidelberg.de/research/groups/biomedical_computer_vision.html) and the [University of Freiburg](https://galaxyproject.eu/freiburg/).

The service is part of the European Galaxy server and is maintained by the [RNA Bioinformatics Center (RBC)](https://www.denbi.de/network/rna-bioinformatics-center-rbc) as part of [de.NBI](https://www.denbi.de) and [ELIXIR](http://elixir-europe.org).
