Welcome to **Galaxy Proteomics**

<em>This European Galaxy branches off from the main <a href="https://usegalaxy.eu">usegalaxy.eu</a> server and focuses on one specific domain: Proteomics.</em>

<strong>Galaxy Proteomics</strong> is a multiple-'omics' data-analysis platform with particular emphasis on mass spectrometry-based proteomics. It is hosted by GalaxyEU and maintained by the <a href="http://galaxyp.org/people/">GalaxyP team</a> from the University of Minnesota, USA.
