## Evolving FAIR Image Analysis in Galaxy for Cross-domain & AI-ready Applications

At this year‚Äôs **BioHackathon Europe**, Project 9 brought together a multidisciplinary, cross-RI team to advance FAIR image analysis in Galaxy, focusing on interoperability, reusability, and AI‚Äëreadiness. The project connected members of Euro‚ÄëBioImaging, ELIXIR, and beyond.

## Why this project?

Image analysis is still fragmented between domains, infrastructures, repositories, and tools. Our goal? Simple but ambitious:  
Make image analysis more **interoperable, reusable, and scalable in Galaxy**, while staying **FAIR** from data to results.

## What we achieved

Concrete results from the hackathon:

- **BioImage Archive ‚Üí Galaxy integration**
  - Added *BioStudies upload* support
  - Improved *BIA retrieval* tooling
- **ISCC support in Galaxy**
  - ISCC hash generator
  - Tools for hash verification and comparison in the making
- **Metadata & standards**
  - Extended **GeoJSON** support
  - Advanced **EDAM Bioimaging** maturity
    - Tool and tutorial annotation
    - Domain-specific term enrichment

Together, these improvements strengthen provenance, traceability, and FAIRness across Galaxy image analysis workflows.
