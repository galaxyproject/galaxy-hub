# Outline

The MaterialVital-Digital Project aims to support the digitalization of processes for the development of Engineered Living Materials (ELMs). To this end processes will be semantically described using Ontologies and experimental work will be reported in ELNs. Galaxy will be leveraged to integrate ELM-related tools and design workflows.
Integration of ELN with galaxy will facilitate data transfer between Galaxy and ELNs like RSpace. Additional integration with CKAN will be leveraged to store generated data.

# Workshop aim

During this 2 Day workshop, INM presented user stories for ELM development, that helped to understand the digitalization needs of the project.
