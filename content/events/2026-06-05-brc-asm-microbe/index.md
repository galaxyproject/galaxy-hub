![infographic for open community forum at ASM-Microbe](https://pathogendatanetwork.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/2026_Open-Community-Forum_flyer.png)

Join Mike Schatz (Johns Hopkins University) from BRC-Analytics and a leader in the Galaxy community for an Open Community Forum at ASM-Microbe on June 5, 2026 at 6:15. This is a town hall event for infectious disease data and bioinformatics. Plans are for spotlight talks on BRC tools and features, a panel discussion on community needs, and networking opportunities.

For more information, visit the [information page at the Pathogen Data Network](https://pathogendatanetwork.org/network/).
