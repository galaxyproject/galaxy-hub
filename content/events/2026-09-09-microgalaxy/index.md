***The [microGalaxy community](https://galaxyproject.org/community/sig/small-scale-admins/) is a [Galaxy community of practice / Special Interest Group (SIG)](https://galaxyproject.org/community/sig/#communities-of-practice) focusing on microbial analysis with Galaxy.***

The next microGalaxy meeting will be held **online** on **Wednesday, September 9th**, at **1 pm UTC / 3 pm CEST / 9 am EDT** ([see your timezone](https://arewemeetingyet.com/UTC/2026-09-09/13:00/microGalaxy)):

**Agenda**:

- Community news and updates (from all of you!)
- A talk by **Tyler and Natalie** on [**BioDIGs**](https://biodigs.org/#home), a US collaborative project exploring the connections between the soil microbiome, the environment, and health ([paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-025-02442-5))
- Discussion on how we can **align our efforts and avoid duplication**.
