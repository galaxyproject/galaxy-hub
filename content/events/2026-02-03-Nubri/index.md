# Kick off event for Galaxy NUBRI

Galaxy NUBRI is a web-based, open platform of the **National University Biomedical Research Institute (NUBRI)**, National Universityâ€“Sudan, operated with support from usegalaxy.eu. 
It provides cloud-hosted bioinformatics and genomics tools and workflows that make computational research accessible, reproducible, and transparent for researchers, students, and partner institutions.
During the webinar, we will introduce Galaxy in general and the subdomain NUBRI in detail.
