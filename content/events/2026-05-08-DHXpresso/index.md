# Join us and enter the Galaxy:

Do you want to digitally analyze your media, texts, or data, but don't know how? 
Do you only have a laptop, but no programming skills and no money for expensive software or HPC computing? 
Are you interested in reproducible research, but don't know where to start?
Join us and enter the Galaxy!

This introduction gives you a quick overview of how you can use the open-source platform Galaxy (usegalaxy.eu) for your research in the Digital Humanities and Social Sciences. 
We will first introduce you to Galaxy and what the platform can do, give examples of how to apply it and then show how you can use the Open Educational Resources from the Galaxy Training Network to learn more. 
All from within your browser. 

For more information on how to attend, please refer to [this website](https://gwdg.de/hpc/events/dhxpresso/).
