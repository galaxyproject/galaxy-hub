## Needed Tools

Uses many "standard" tools that exist on most Galaxy servers, and

* FastQC
* One of the FASTQ quality filters
* A short read mapper (e.g., Bwa or Bowtie)
* SAMtools
* GATK
* IGV or Trackster
* o8 Variant Visualizer (online)
